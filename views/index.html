Hadoop Platform and Application Framework

Offered By: Coursera
Completion Date: Still Ongoing

Students are also introduced to the Hadoop Stack, also known as the Hadoop Zoo, Hadoop Distributed File System (HDFS), MapReduce as well as Spark (PySpark).

Thus, this course serves as a refresher for me as I revisit Hadoop / Spark.

Yale: Financial Markets

Offered By: Coursera
Completion Date: 2023

The Financial Markets course is an introductory course towards the world of finance. It introduces many basic financial concepts such as market risk & efficiency, portfolio management practices, stock properties etc., while also drawing links between human behavior and their historic effect on influencing modern day market policies and trends (Behavioral Economics). It is taught by Robert J. Schiller a world renowned economist, whose publications has earned him major awards such as the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economics and the Deutsche Bank Prize.

While this is not a CS related course, as someone who was pursuing a career in the finance sector, I believed that it was imperative that I, at the very least, understood some commonly used "Finance Jargon", as well as financial concepts in order for me to better appreciate my work and allow me to better contribute to projects that I will be working on, especially considering the fact that I did not have any background in finance.

Skills: Behavioral Finance and Economics, Market Risk, Market Theory, Portfolio Management, Financial Regulations
